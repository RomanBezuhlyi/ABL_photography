FAQ

  • Do I need to bring my own props & accessories to our session?

    We will provide every prop and accessory we’ll need for your session! Over a dozen different blankets, dozens of baskets, over 50 hats, TONS of headbands! Of course if you have any items you would like to incorporate into your session, we encourage you to bring them as we’d love to create shots that are personal to you! Another HUGE benefit of choosing us for your newborn session… CUSTOMIZED props just for you! From hats to headbands to wraps! Have an idea for a hat or a specific color combination you’d like to have in your session? Let us know during your consultation and we’ll make sure to have it ready just for your session when you come in!

  • When should I book my newborn session?

    Once your newborn is here, we suggest having someone contact us immediately so we can choose a date for your session. To insure the sleepy, curly, newborn poses, we advise scheduling a newborn session within the first 14 days after delivery. Parents of breastfed babies should wait until the mother’s milk comes in so your newborn will be sure to have a nice full tummy after every feeding (about 7-10 days). Bottle fed babies generally take more per feeding so may be photographed as early as 4-5 days old.

  • How long will my newborn photography session take?

    Between soothing, precise posing, feeding, and setting up/prepping our shots, our newborn photography sessions take about 3 hours. Multiple newborns may take a little bit longer. We love taking our time to perfect every picture we create.

  • How do I prepare for my newborn photography session?

    We put together a set of guidelines that can help your newborn session go as smooth as possible. You will receive it the day before your session.

  • Can siblings and family participate in the newborn session?

    Yes. We focus on the baby at the beginning of the session and take sibling and family portraits at the end of the session. We recommend bringing siblings for the last hour of your 3 hour studio session. So, they dont have to sit in the studio for the whole 3 hour session.

  • How does it work?

    • Step 1 - Get in touch! If you are interested in booking a session, please contact us using the form below to inquire about availability and ask any other initial questions you might have.
    • Step 2 - Reserve your session We will send you an email containing more information about the session you are interested in, and how to schedule and reserve your spot. There is a deposit/session fee required at the time of booking.
    • Step 3 - Time for your session! We will custom design your session, prepare beautiful setups for your baby and take care of all the details. You just have to bring your baby to the studio, sit back, relax and watch us do our magic or take a nap on the comfortable couch.
    • Step 4 - Choose your photos You will come back to the studio for your ordering appointment where you can see your amazing pictures, select your favorites and decide on the package. You can purchase digital images and order archival prints, albums and products.
